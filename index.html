Events

IBW - Indian Blockchain Week
25 December 2024
India Blockchain Week 2024 in Bangalore featured major blockchain and Web3 conferences, hackathons, and networking, attracting global leaders and innovators, and highlighting the city’s role in India’s Web3 growth.
Build- It Tour 2.0
19th August 2025
BuildIt Tour 2.0 (2025) at Microsoft Bangalore was an exciting tech event by HackwithIndia and Reskilll, focused on AI, blockchain, and innovation. It featured expert talks, workshops, and hands-on demos with Microsoft tools like Azure AI. The event fostered learning, networking, and collaboration among developers and students, inspiring new ideas and growth in India’s tech community.
